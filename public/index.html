Potential Solutions

Server-Side YouTube Proxy

Create a server-side endpoint that proxies requests to YouTube, avoiding direct client-side access that might be blocked.

Alternative Video Sources

Consider adding support for other video platforms that may have less restrictive cloud hosting policies.

CORS Configuration

Ensure your server is sending proper CORS headers to allow cross-origin requests from your frontend.
